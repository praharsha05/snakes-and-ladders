📖 RULEBOOK
💻
MILLENNIAL MODE
Classic rules. Pure strategy. No chaos — just you vs the board.
🎯 Objective
🏆
Be the first player to reach cell 100 exactly. The board goes 1–100.
🎲 Turn Structure
🎲
Tap ROLL to roll a die (1–6). Your token advances that many cells.
6️⃣
Roll a 6 and you get an extra turn immediately!
🚫
If your roll would take you past 100, you don't move — wait for the exact number.
🐍 Snakes & 🪜 Ladders
🪜
Land on the bottom of a ladder → climb up to a higher cell.
🐍
Land on a snake's head → slide down to its tail cell.
📌
Snakes and ladders are fixed — their positions never change.
👥 Players
👤
Supports 2–4 players. Players take turns in order.
💡
Millennial Mode is great for beginners or a quick casual game!
📱
GEN Z MODE
Chaotic. Unpredictable. The board reshuffles every single turn.
🎯 Objective
🏆
Same as Millennial — reach cell 100 first to win.
⚡ The Chaos Rule
⚡
Before every roll, all snakes and ladders RESHUFFLE to new random positions.
🗺️
The board you see is always changing — a safe spot now might be dangerous next turn.
🎲
After the reshuffle, roll normally. The chaos happens automatically.
📐 Board Rules
🐍
Always exactly 6 snakes and 6 ladders on the board every reshuffle.
🔒
Cells 1 and 100 are always safe — no snake/ladder endpoints placed there.
🎲 Dice & Turns
6️⃣
Roll a 6 → extra turn (board reshuffles again for that turn too!).
🚫
Cannot exceed cell 100 — need the exact roll to win.
💡
Memorising the board won't help here! React fast and embrace the unpredictability.
🤖
GEN α MODE
Bid. Bluff. Control. The board is yours — if you can afford it.
🎯 Objective
🏆
First player to reach cell 100 wins and earns BONUS COINS.
⭐
Once you reach 100, you become a spectator — game continues for others.
💰 Coins
💰
Each player starts with 200 coins (or their saved balance from previous games).
🏦
Coins are saved permanently — earn them in any mode, spend them in Gen α.
🎁
The winner earns 150+ BONUS COINS added to their wallet.
🎭 Secret Bidding
🤫
Before the game, each player privately bids coins on SNAKES and LADDERS they want to control.
👁️
Bids are completely hidden — only the current price is visible to others.
🏅
The highest bidder wins control of that snake or ladder. All bidders pay their amount.
🎭
The winner secretly decides: Keep original behavior, or Invert it (snake ↔ ladder).
❓ Uncertain Behavior
🐍
A SNAKE might actually act as a ladder — nobody knows until someone lands on it!
🪜
A LADDER might send you down like a snake — true behavior is hidden until interaction.
📢
On landing, a reveal card shows what it acted as and who controlled it.
🔄 Mid-Game Rebidding
💰
Tap 💰 REBID during the game to bid on items again. New bids must exceed the current price.
🚫
Rebidding is blocked if any player is currently on a snake/ladder endpoint.
💡
Bluff with your bids! Control high-value snakes near 100 to sabotage leaders, or snag a key ladder early.
